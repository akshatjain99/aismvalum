AISMV ALUM ASSOCIATION

About Us

Who we are

The AISMV Alum Association was formed with the aim of connecting with our Alma Mater.
We do so primarily by sharing our experiences and skills we learned after graduating - in the form of workshops and events with the student body of AISMV.

Our Origin Story

It all began in April, 2015. A group of pass-outs on Facebook wondered how grand it would be to organize a reunion in school. They created a Google Form. Over 300 alum showed interest in attending. Almost eight months later, the first Alum Meet was organized on Christmas Eve. Over 200 people attended.

But, that wasn't enough. Alum from all batches wondered if they could organize themselves into an Alum Association and formally connect with the school. And here we are!
