Latest Events

AMVMUN 2016

AMVMUN 2016 consisted of 5 committees and saw participation of over 200 students. Satrajit Sahani served as Prime Minister in Research and Analysis Wing, and Aaknhi Anwesha was the president of the European Union. Advik Aggarwal also played a key role in training the students - sharing wisdom with delegates about key aspects of debating.

CyberCon 2016

“Cybercon – Invoke the future” is the flagship intra-school event of the IT society, Metaverse. 200 zealous participants. Ten categories such as Presentations, Video Editing, Photography spread over two days. The result: school raging with enthusiasm for technology and learning.
