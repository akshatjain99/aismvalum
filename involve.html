Get Involved

What we've done till now

In the past one year, we've worked closely with the school to identify key areas where the Alum can pitch in. Some of these are: mentoring societies, conducting workshops and organizing guidance sessions. We also organize Alum Meets annually that is open for all batches to attend.

Work with us

We're constantly looking out for experienced individuals who can conduct workshops in school and help moderate our Facebook Group. We're just getting started so watch this space for more opportunities coming up soon.

Societies & Alum Mentors

You can interact with the newly launched clubs and societies in school through a variety of ways. The societies and clubs give students a platform to build their skills and showcase their unique talents. The structure of a society has been created so that interactive sessions, workshops and events can be organized. Each society also organizes its flagship event once an year.

We are also looking for alum mentors for the Academic Year 2017-18. Alum Mentors guide students as prepare for inter-school, national and international competitions. You can also get in touch to conduct workshops in collaboration with the relevant society.

Here's a list of some societies you can interact with:

  • Astitva : Life Skills Society
  • Cynosure : Literary society
  • Crescendo :Western Music Society
  • Hive : Applied Sciences Society
  • Genesis: Mass Communication and Publication Society
  • Metaverse: Cyber Society
  • Symphony : Performing Arts Society
  • Veritas: International Affairs Society
  • Zeal : Sports Society
