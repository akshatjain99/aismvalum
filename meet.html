FAQs

Do I need to Register for the Alum Association first?

Of course. Click here to register. If you registered last year, you do NOT need to do it again. We hate forms as much as you do.

Will the dates be changed?

No. The dates have been finalized by the School. No possibility of a change here.

Is there anything I should keep in mind before coming?

Remember the sanctity of the School. If you come to the Alum Meet in an improper condition (*you know what I mean*), you’ll be thrown out in front of all your friends and you’ll be banned from entering the premises EVER AGAIN. You’re not a child anymore, please be responsible.

Why isn’t the Alum Meet free?

The Event Amount you pay goes towards getting really nice Food, having some sweet decorations and into other things. No one benefits economically from this event. The entire Amount is used to create a nice Event for YOU. If you’d like to know the breakdown of how the Amount shall be used, talk to us on the day of the event

Can I pay using Cash/Cheque?

Nopes. You can however ask a friend to pay your Event Amount with your Name and Contact Details, in case you do not have a Credit/Debit Card. You can also pay using Net Banking.

I want to say something/sing/dance/perform. How can I get in touch with you?

Drop us a message on the Facebook Page and we’ll try to accommodate you in the informal event.

Is there a dress code?

Yes! Formals and Semi-formals work. For women: Ethnic or trousers, shirt + jacket/sweater. For men: Ethnic, suit, or trousers,shirt + jacket/sweater. The essence is: Wear something nice, comfortable and warm :) (No dressses or hoodies, please)

Can I pay on 23rd itself?

No. Walk-ins are NOT be allowed.

Who am I paying this money to?

The money goes into Ankita Marwaha’s Account. This is temporary till we get our own bank account set up in the name of the Association. Plus Ankita di is a CA, so she knows how to handle money.

Is there a chance of the event getting cancelled?

Na-uh. Everything's set!

Is the Alum Association organizing any After Party scenes?

No. Like last year, w're sure all of you will have plans with your school friends. Have a great time!

I’m having troubles with the Payment Gateway. What do I do?

Just send us a message on the Facebook Page and we’ll sort it out.

My question isn't in here!

No worries. Let's talk on the Facebook Page. Just send us a message and we'll be glad to help you.
