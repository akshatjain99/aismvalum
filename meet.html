FAQs

Do I need to Register for the Alum Association first?

Of course. Click here to register.

Will the dates be changed?

No.The dates have been finalized by the School. No possibility of a change here

Why isn’t the Alum Meet free?

The Event Amount you pay goes towards getting really nice Food, having some sweet decorations and into other things. NO ONE benefits economically from this event. The entire Amount is used to create a nice Event FOR YOU. If you’d like to know the breakdown of how the Amount shall be used, talk to us on the day of the event

Can I pay using Cash/Cheque?

Nopes. Ask a friend to pay your Event Amount with your Name and Contact Details if you do not have a Credit/Debit Card. Net Banking Works too.

I want to say something/sing/dance/perform. How can I get in touch with you?

Drop us a message on the Facebook Page and we’ll try to accommodate you in our schedule
